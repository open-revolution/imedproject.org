Following the publication of our first report: the [Horizon Scan Report](/research/horizon-scan) and accompanying review of the most important remuneration rights style proposals to date [Proposals Database](/research/proposals-database). We are pleased to announce that 20 of these proposals have been individually summarised and added to this website (see the list bellow).

Each summary reviews and contrasts existing and potential proposals and their funding mechanisms for pharmaceutical research and development.
